A game about survival in an industrial post apocalyptic world

This game aims to make suffer the pain you will feel in a real post apocalyptic world.
It's developed with scenarios like chernobyl or fukushima in mind, with its devastation and loneliness that make you feel like this world doesn't exist anymore.

New scripting technology

This game was developed with the brand new development by substraction that aims to do only things that are necessary.
Planning all at first and secondly taking apart all innecesary stuff from the code, making a game with an impressive performance.
